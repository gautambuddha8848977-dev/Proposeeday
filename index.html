For my queen

My Queen,

I don’t come to you today with a question, because my heart has never doubted its answer. I come to you with gratitude — the kind that lives quietly inside me and feels heavier than words. Loving you has been the most natural thing my soul has ever done.

You are not just the woman I love. You are the calm in my chaos, the warmth in my ordinary days, and the strength I lean on without even realizing it. Life feels softer simply because you are in it.

I admire you more than I ever say. Your heart, your patience, your grace — the way you carry yourself like a queen without ever needing a crown. Being loved by you is a privilege I will never take lightly.

On this Propose Day, I don’t ask you to be mine — because you already are, and I am already yours. Instead, I promise to keep choosing you, protecting us, and growing with you through every season.

“I propose my forever to you — again and always.”
Happy Propose Day, my wife.
